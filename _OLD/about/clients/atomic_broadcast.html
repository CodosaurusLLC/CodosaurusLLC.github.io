Who are they?

Atomic Broadcast no longer exists.  It was a very early-stage startup, with an idea about some social media filtering.  (No I won't tell you more.)  There was, as far as I know, only the founder, and no code written before me. 

Also, this was my first Rails freelancing gig!  :-)

What did I do, when, where?

I created a tiny Rails app for them with one feature: retrieval of a user's tweets from Twitter, via the Twitter API.  This may sound trivial, but it was supposed to be a "test drive", possibly leading to later work if things worked out and he got funding for the rest of his idea.  I did this in under the four hours allotted, in December 2011, 100% remotely.
