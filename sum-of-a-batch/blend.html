I want to blend a batch of mead with another batch, or with something else, like water for dilution, or juice or honey for back-sweetening.

The first one has:

  • units (gallons, liters, litres, whatever) of volume*
  • specific gravity*
  • % alcohol by volume

The second one has:

  • units of volume (same units as above)*
  • specific gravity*
  • % alcohol by volume
* required values, must be greater than zero

Later I may add:

  • blending more than two batches
  • picking batches from a dropdown list of your batches*
  • picking batches from a list of search results*
*If I wind up adding saving of batches at all; see "Plan a Batch!".
